Frequently Asked Questions

What is the life of a rooftop solar system?

A rooftop solar system typically has a life of 25-30 years. The performance of the panels degrades slightly over time, but they continue to generate electricity for decades.

Do Solar Rooftop projects have a high maintenance cost?

No, the maintenance cost for solar rooftop projects is generally very low. The main requirement is to keep the panels clean, which can be done easily with water and a soft brush.

Can Solar projects damage my roof?

When installed correctly by professionals, solar projects do not damage your roof. Our installation process ensures the structural integrity of your roof is maintained.

Do I need to clean my Solar plant?

Regular cleaning of solar panels is recommended to maintain optimal efficiency. Dust, dirt, and debris can reduce power generation.

My roof is not made of concrete; can I still install a Solar Project?

Yes, solar panels can be installed on various types of rooftops, including metal and tiled roofs. Our engineers will assess your specific roof type and provide a suitable solution.
